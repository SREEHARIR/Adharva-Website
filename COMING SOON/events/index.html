Events

 

Adharva talk



Adharva’16 presents a stage talk show - to listen, to interact and to put forth one’s field of vision. Grab the opportunity to meet celebrities and get to listen to their inspirational aspects. This is the best open discussion that you can ever get with a popular star. Who knows, it might even be your favourite icon! Don’t miss this golden chance to have a memorable discourse with a personality notorious for fame!

Mad Ads



Marketing is an art possessed by few and desired by all. Indeed, with the right words and gestures, a pair of worn-out shoes can be made appealing to the eyes of the customer. Your creativity and charisma can give you an edge over the other advertisers. Here’s the challenge we place before you – how fast can you sell the goods for sale?

Photography



A picture is worth thousand words, and sometimes a picture is all that is needed to convey a hundred meanings. Indeed, the contraction of a few facial muscles is the difference between a sweet smile and an angry frown. The challenge we have put forth to all budding photographers is to capture all these subtle changes of the human face in three hours. Can your camera lenses pick the subtlety in the human visage that our eyes fail to see? Time to test your skills to be near the right person at the right time!

RC Racing



Navigation is never an easy experience, but how well can you build a machine that can run equally well on pits, lines and curves, and still be ready to take on more? IEEE MBCET, jointly with Adharva’16 invites all budding engineers to develop a remote-controlled machine consistent in its ability to tackle all types of terrain. Can your machine make it to the top by achieving the fastest time in our racecourse? Filled with surprise elements, this event is a dare to all those willing to put their technical skills to the ultimate test.

Best Startup



Everything starts with an idea. The strength of an idea is based on its originality, relevance and potential to revolutionise the world as we know it. Do you have such a brainwave that can take the world by storm? Such a venture is impossible without a group of like-minded people working towards the same objective, so here’s an opportunity to put together your start-up to really see what you are up against in the real world! Put together a business presentation and let’s see if you get prospective investors. Doesn’t matter if you are new to the world of business and pitching your ideas – all you need is persistence and charisma to win people over. Are you up for this challenge?
