Perfect Ambassador

Contest . Convince . Conquer



People have different perceptions. Some read between the lines. Some think out of the box. Others define the box. To get your ideas into the hearts and minds of people, one must have the ability to think in all these angles and see the same view in the eyes of others. Can your bold persuasion work its magic by making people believe what you want them to? You may be as cunning as Shylock or as wilful as Portia, but you need the abilities of both to win this contest. Let's shift the gears and accelerate through the souls of people! Get ready to shatter the boundaries between different opinions and become the perfect ambassador!

Rules

  • This is an individual contest.
  • There is no restriction on the number of entries per college.
  • There will be four rounds for the competition.
  • Judging of performance would be based on presentation skills, communication skills, body language, creative skills and general knowledge.
  • Violation of any rules and disrespectful conduct can lead to disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges is final.

    • Round 1: The Questionnaire

  • Participants would be given a questionnaire which should be filled by them individually.
  • Criteria of judgement: creativity, originality and spontaneity of answers
  • Note: This would be a screening round depending on the number of participants.

    • Round 2: Present yourself

  • Participants have to introduce themselves to the judges in as unique way possible.
  • There is no criterion for this round â€“ just impress the judges!

    • Round 3: Make it Sell

  • Participants would be given a company at random, with the necessary information about the company.
  • Proving themselves to be the brand ambassadors of the company, they should present the company in an effective manner.
  • There is also an interaction with judges and audience regarding the company they have to represent.
  • Criteria of judgement: communication skills, proficiency in language, speaking, body language

    • Round 4: The Bonus Round

  • This is a surprise round that we would be revealing on the Day.
